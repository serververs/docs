## Get Started with Serververse

Serververse makes hosting simple, reliable, and fast. This guide walks you through everything — from purchasing the right service to getting online.

## Step 2 — Purchase Your Service

1. Go to the [Serververse Store](https://store.serververs.com)
2. Select your plan  
3. Complete checkout — provisioning is instant

---

## Step 3 — Access Your Panel

- **Web Hosting:** CyberPanel — manage files, SSL, domains, and databases  
- **Minecraft Servers:** Pterodactyl Panel — start/stop server, upload mods/plugins  
- **Cloud VPS:** Proxmox — console access, snapshots, reinstalls  
- **Enterprise:** Custom portal or dedicated account manager

You can access all panels from:  
[https://serververs.com/panel](https://serververs.com/panel)
